# Fast Track

Enjoy your Plantree in **10 minutes**!

## Step 1: Deploy on Vercel

Deploy your Plantree project with one click:

[![Deploy with Vercel](https://vercel.com/button)](https://vercel.com/new/clone?repository-url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fplantree-xyz%2Fplantree&env=DATABASE_URL&envDescription=Your+Personal+Database+Url&envLink=https%3A%2F%2Fdocs.plantree.xyz%2Fguides%2Fenvironment-variables%23database_url&project-name=my-plantree-blog&repository-name=my-plantree-blog&fork=true)

<Steps>

{<h3>Connect to GitHub</h3>}

Ensure your GitHub account is connected to Vercel. If it's already connected, you can skip this step:

![vercel-deploy-connect-git-provider](/images/screenshot/vercel-deploy-connect-git-provider.png)

{<h3>Clone repository</h3>}

Select the Git scope and enter your desired repository name:

![vercel-deploy-clone-repository](/images/screenshot/vercel-deploy-clone-repository.png)

{<h3>Bind Database</h3>}

Enter your PostgreSQL connection URL (`DATABASE_URL`). If you haven't set up a database yet, 
refer to our [guide](/guides/create-vercel-postgres) to create a PostgreSQL Database on Vercel.

A typical `DATABASE_URL` looks like this:

```text
postgres://username:password@hostname:port/database_name?sslmode=require
```

Once you've configured the necessary environment variables, click the deploy button:

![vercel-deploy-config-env](/images/screenshot/vercel-deploy-config-env.png)

{<h3>Await deployment</h3>}

Deployment will take a few minutes. Please be patient.

![vercel-deploy-wait](/images/screenshot/vercel-deploy-wait.png)

{<h3>Deployment completed</h3>}

Congratulations! Your Plantree project is now live on Vercel. You can directly open the preview to visit your site, or configure your custom domain for access.

![vercel-depoly-compeleted](/images/screenshot/vercel-depoly-compeleted.png)

</Steps>

## Step 2: Create ðŸŒ± **TreeId**

<Callout type="info">
  TreeId is your personal "key" to the Web3 world
</Callout>

Follow these steps to create your TreeId:

1. Visit [plantree.xyz](https://www.plantree.xyz/create-space).
2. **Connect** your wallet and **sign** the request.
3. **Complete** the registration form with the required information.
4. Click the **Create site** button to finalize your TreeId creation.

![web3-tree-id-create](/images/screenshot/web3-tree-id-create.png)

Upon successful creation, your TreeId will be displayed below:

![web3-tree-id-view](/images/screenshot/web3-tree-id-view.png)

## Step 3: Bind ðŸŒ± **TreeId**

To bind your TreeId to your Plantree website, follow these steps:

1. Navigate to your **personal Plantree website**. If you've forgotten the link, you can find it in your Vercel Dashboard.
2. **Connect** your wallet to log in.
3. Click on your **avatar** in the top corner.
4. Select **Settings** from the dropdown menu.
5. Enter your TreeId in the designated field and submit it.

![plantree-setting-tree-id](/images/screenshot/plantree-setting-tree-id.png)
