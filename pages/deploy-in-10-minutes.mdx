# Deploy in 10 mimutes

**You can deploy PenX in various ways, such as:**

- [Deploy to Vercel](/deployment/vercel)
- [Deploy to Fly.io](deployment/flyio)
- [Deploy to Railway](deployment/railway)
- [Deploy with Docker ](/deployment/docker)
- [Deploy with Docker Compose](/deployment/docker-compose)

Here is the guide for deploying to Vercel:

<Callout type="info">
  You will need to prepare a [GitHub](https://github.com/signup?source=login) account and a [Vercel](https://vercel.com/login) account before proceeding.
</Callout>

<Steps>

{<h3>Step1: Click Deploy Button</h3>}

ðŸ‘‡ Click to start 

[![Deploy with Vercel](https://vercel.com/button)](https://vercel.com/new/clone?repository-url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fpenx-lab%2Fpenx&env=DATABASE_URL&envDescription=Your+Personal+Database+Url&envLink=https%3A%2F%2Fdocs.penx.io%2Fguides%2Fenvironment-variables%23database_url&project-name=my-penx-blog&repository-name=my-penx-blog&fork=true)

Vercel will clone the [Penx]('https://github.com/penx-lab/penx') and deploy the site for you.

{<h3>Step2: Compelete On Vercel</h3>}

After clicking the deploy button, you'll be redirected to the Vercel page. Don't worry! We've preconfigured the necessary parameters and settings for you.

1. choose your **git providers**.

![vercel-deploy-connect-git-provider](/images/screenshot/vercel-deploy-connect-git-provider.png)

2. check **git scope** and **repository name**.

![vercel-deploy-clone-repository](/images/screenshot/vercel-deploy-clone-repository.png)

3. configure the **DATABASE URL**.

<Callout type="info">
  If you don't have a URL yet, refer to this [guide](/get-a-database) to get a
  PostgreSQL database.
</Callout>

![vercel-deploy-config-env](/images/screenshot/vercel-deploy-config-env.png)

here is a example:

```text
postgresql://user:demo@43.154.135.183:5437/demo
```

4. click the **deploy button** and waiting for compeleted.

![vercel-deploy-wait](/images/screenshot/vercel-deploy-wait.png)

Congratulations! The service has been successfully deployed.

![vercel-depoly-compeleted](/images/screenshot/vercel-depoly-compeleted.png)

{<h3>Step3: Create SpaceId</h3>}

<Callout type="info">SpaceId is your personal "key" to the Web3 world.</Callout>

To create your SpaceId, visit [penx.io](https://www.penx.io/create-space) and fill out the application form:

![web3-tree-id-create](/images/screenshot/web3-tree-id-create.png)

Once created, your SpaceId will be displayed below:

![web3-tree-id-view](/images/screenshot/web3-tree-id-view.png)

{<h3>Step4: Bind SpaceId</h3>}

Bind SpaceId and activate the Web3 features of your blog. Here is the steps:

1. Open your personal PenX website and **log in**.
2. Clicking on your **avatar** and selecting the **Dashboard** menu.
3. Find the **Settings** section.
4. In the **Web3** configuration area, enter your SpaceId.
5. Click **Save**.

![penx-setting-tree-id](/images/screenshot/penx-setting-tree-id.png)

</Steps>
