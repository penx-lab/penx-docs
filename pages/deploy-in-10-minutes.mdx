# Deploy in 10 mimutes

Enjoy your PenX in **10 minutes**!

**You can deploy PenX in various ways, such as:**

- [Deploy to Vercel](/deployment/vercel)
- [Deploy to Fly.io](deployment/flyio)
- [Deploy with Docker ](/deployment/docker)
- [Deploy with Docker Compose](/deployment/docker-compose)

Here is the guide for deploying to Vercel:

<Steps>

{<h3>Click Deploy Button</h3>}

ðŸ‘‡ Start your deployment on [Vercel](https://vercel.com/):

[![Deploy with Vercel](https://vercel.com/button)](https://vercel.com/new/clone?repository-url=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fpenx-lab%2Fpenx&env=DATABASE_URL&envDescription=Your+Personal+Database+Url&envLink=https%3A%2F%2Fdocs.penx.io%2Fguides%2Fenvironment-variables%23database_url&project-name=my-penx-blog&repository-name=my-penx-blog&fork=true)

{<h3>Create Git Repository</h3>}

After accessing Vercel's deployment page, you'll be prompted to select a Git repository:

<Callout type="warning" emoji="âš ï¸">
  If your Vercel account is not yet linked to GitHub, you will be redirected to
  complete the linking process.
</Callout>

![vercel-deploy-connect-git-provider](/images/screenshot/vercel-deploy-connect-git-provider.png)

Once linking is complete, click the **Create** button:

![vercel-deploy-clone-repository](/images/screenshot/vercel-deploy-clone-repository.png)

{<h3>Configure Project</h3>}

Now, configure the necessary parameters to run the service.

You'll need a PostgreSQL connection URL, which looks like this:

```text
postgres://username:password@hostname:port/database_name?sslmode=require
```

<Callout type="info">
  If you don't have a URL yet, refer to this [guide](/get-a-database) to get a
  PostgreSQL database.
</Callout>

Enter the details in the designated fields as shown below, then click **Deploy** to proceed:

![vercel-deploy-config-env](/images/screenshot/vercel-deploy-config-env.png)

{<h3>Deploy</h3>}

Deployment will take a few minutes. Please be patient:

![vercel-deploy-wait](/images/screenshot/vercel-deploy-wait.png)

Congratulations! The service has been successfully deployed. Only two steps remain: create your TreeId and bind it to your PenX website:

![vercel-depoly-compeleted](/images/screenshot/vercel-depoly-compeleted.png)

{<h3>Create TreeId</h3>}

<Callout type="info">TreeId is your personal "key" to the Web3 world.</Callout>

To create your TreeId, visit [penx.io](https://www.penx.io/create-space) and fill out the application form:

![web3-tree-id-create](/images/screenshot/web3-tree-id-create.png)

Once created, your TreeId will be displayed below:

![web3-tree-id-view](/images/screenshot/web3-tree-id-view.png)

{<h3>Bind TreeId</h3>}

Final step: Open your personal PenX website. Once logged in, click on your avatar to access the settings page.
In the Web3 configuration section, enter your TreeId and click **Save**:

![penx-setting-tree-id](/images/screenshot/penx-setting-tree-id.png)

</Steps>
