## General

Customize these general settings to personalize your site:

- **Avatar**: Upload an avatar for display.
- **Site Name**: Set your website's title.
- **Brief Introduction**: Add a short introduction about your site.
- **About**: Provide detailed information about your mission.

## Web3

<Callout emoji="â—">
  This configuration is crucial.
</Callout>

Adjust the Web3 settings to enable advanced features:

- **TreeID**: Required contract address for Web3 functionality. Obtain your TreeID [here](https://www.plantree.xyz/create-space). For [more details](/guides/Onchain-plantree).

## Auth Type

Choose your preferred authentication method:

- **Reown (default)**: Secure Web3 wallet for decentralized identity management.
- **Privy**: Privacy-focused Web3 wallet.
- **Google**: Familiar Web2 login method.

## Storage Provider

Select a storage option for your static assets, like images and videos:

- **IPFS (default)**: Decentralized storage ensuring integrity.
- **Vercel Blob**: Scalable storage, perfect for Vercel-hosted projects.
- **Supabase Storage (coming soon)**: Easy integration with Supabase services.
