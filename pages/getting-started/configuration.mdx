## General

In the General settings, you can customize the following:

- **Avatar**: Upload or select an avatar that will be displayed on your website.
- **Site Name**: Set the name that will appear as the title of your website.
- **Brief Introduction**: Write a short introduction about your site or yourself.
- **About**: Provide detailed information about your website or mission.

## Web3

In the Web3 settings, you can customize the following:

- **TreeID**: A contract address required for Plantree's Web3 functionalities.

You can create and get a TreeID [here](https://www.plantree.xyz/create-space). For [more details](/guides/Onchain-plantree).

## Auth Type

You can select different authentication methods, each offering a distinct experience. The available options are:

- **Reown (default)**: A Web3 wallet option that provides secure and decentralized identity management.
- **Privy**: A Web3 wallet option focusing on privacy and data protection.
- **Google**: A Web2 authentication method for easy and familiar login.

## Storage Provider

In the Storage Provider settings, you can choose from the following options to store static assets from your blog content, such as images, audio, and video:

- **IPFS (default)**: A decentralized storage solution ensuring content integrity and resilience.
- **Vercel Blob**: Offers scalable and efficient storage, ideal for projects hosted on Vercel.
- **Supabase Storage (coming soon)**: An upcoming option providing easy integration with Supabase services.

## Socials

In the Socials settings, you can link your profiles to enhance connectivity and engagement. The available options are:

- **Farcaster**
- **X**
- **Facebook**
- **YouTube**
- **Instagram**
- **LinkedIn**
- **GitHub**
