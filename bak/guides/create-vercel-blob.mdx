# Create Vercel Blob

Learn how to create a Blob on Vercel and retrieve its connection string.

## Requirements

First, ensure you have a Vercel account. Click the button below to log in or sign up.

## Create Steps

<Steps>

### Log in Vercel

Log in to Vercel and navigate to the dashboard. Open the **Storage** page.

![vercel-storage-page](/images/screenshot/vercel-storage-page.png)

### Choose Blob

Click the **Create** button, Fill in the **Blob name** and submit the form to finalize your Blob creation.

![vercel-blob-create](/images/screenshot/vercel-blob-create.png)

### Retrieve Secrets

Go to your Blob dashboard to find and note your Blob secret:

![vercel-blob-check-secret](/images/screenshot/vercel-blob-check-secret.png)

</ Steps>
